Keyania is a sound researcher from Dallas.

"These are 8 sounds I vividly remember from my childhood in the late 90s/early 2000s. Similar to the function of an old Polaroid, these invoke memories from formidable experiences in my life as heard through my ears. Welcome to my inner radio." -KC

first memories of the lawless, wonderful internet
Born of a woman, raised by the world wide web.
I couldn’t wait to turn 21 and go to every music festival. Then my local rock radio station broadcast live from Lollapalooza. I recorded them all to tape just in case I really did go one day.
radio station educated me on music played outside the home
My mother is famous for having the longest Christmas voicemail recording. Bless anyone who had to leave us a message.
